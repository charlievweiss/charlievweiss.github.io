Squid! Nov 2024

A custom toy for a baby who is into textures and a mama who is into squids.

×
❮ ❯

I love making toys. It takes a lot of problem solving to figure out the shapes I want, and I love the thrill of making something unique. This particular gem has tentacles made out of a variety of fabrics (flannel, silk, sticky couch material, etc.) so the little recipient can know what they all taste like!
