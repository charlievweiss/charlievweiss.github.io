Lightweight Intelligent Fixed-Wing Technology (LIFT) Lab

Olin College of Engineering

Summer 2016
Link to GitHub

The Olin LIFT (Lightweight Intelligent Fixed-wing Technology) Lab is the collection of projects on the Olin Robotics Lab's fixed-wing planes. I jumpstarted work on these projects in the summer of 2016 with my teammates, Nathan Yee and Jacob Regenstein.

I recommend checking out the GitHub to see the full documentation.
